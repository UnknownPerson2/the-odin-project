Milk Tea Recipe

Description

Brown sugar bubble tea is an iced black tea drink with brown sugar, milk, and tapioca balls (also called boba). The secret to making the best bubble tea is to use warm tapioca balls.

Ingredients

  • Black Tea: Use any kind of black tea you like. The black tea for this drink doesn't need to be the highest quality, so feel free to use tea bags./li>
  • Milk: Although you can use any kind of milk, whole milk makes the drink rich and creamy.
  • Brown Sugar: Brown sugar is a mix of white sugar and molasses, giving the drink a richer flavor than white, granulated sugar.
  • Tapioca Balls: Store-bought tapioca balls are dried, so all you need to do is boil them in water to make them soft and chewy
  • Water & Ice: Preferably filtered water

Steps

  1. Steep tea in hot water.
  2. Cook tapioca balls in brown sugar and water.
  3. Add boba and line brown sugar syrup inside a cup.
  4. Add ice.
  5. Pour in black tea.
  6. Top with milk
